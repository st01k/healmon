CS321 - Mobile Apps

Assignment 9: Health Monitoring App - MySQL Storage

Continue to build on the last chapter's application, that will monitor both blood pressure and body mass index (you can model it on the Thyroid app).

Implement the following:

  • A password-based entry page
  • A page to get basic information about the person (name, dob, health insurance info, and a password reset)
  • A menu page with options and submenus
    • Blood Pressure
      • Enter new data about your blood pressure
      • Update data about your blood pressure
      • A graph of the blood pressure data entered so far
      • A recommendations page to make recommendations based on the values of your blood pressure
    • BMI
      • Enter new data about your height and weight
      • Update data about our BMI
      • A graph of your height, weight, and BMI
      • A recommendations page to make recommendations based on the values of your BMI
    • User Settings
      • Update personal information
      • Change graph type for BP and BMI
